About Us

Welcome to COFFEESSEUR, your local Coffee Connoisseurs of Puncak Alam!

We, as fellow cafe hoppers, are here to provide you a full directory of cafes that are established in the Puncak Alam Area

We provide a directory of Chain Cafes and locally owned cafes!

COFFEESSEUR is here to make sure you make the right choice in picking your new favourite coffee spot through reviews that we have collected online

VISION AND MISSION

Our Mission in COFFEESSEUR is to provide fellow residents with information and a simple, reliable, and user-friendly platform about the various cafes that are established around Puncak Alam

Our Vision is to become the leading coffee directory platform in Puncak Alam by connecting peopel with great coffee experiences while supporting the local cafes community

Our History

COFFEESSEUR was first founded and developed in 2026 when we discovered that there are a HUGE number of cafes in the Puncak Alam area

BUT! We know basically nothing about them without having to go and find out ourselves.

This is exactly why COFFEESSEUR cafe directory is established. We want to provide our fellow coffee lovers the various options of cafes and coffee spots that they might enjoy together with what the locak customers might say

To see more of the cafes that can be seen in this site, users can click on the logos of the cafes and they will directly be brought to the cafe's website or social media account

We at COFFEESSEUR hope that with this service you'll be able to find your new favourite chill coffee spot!
